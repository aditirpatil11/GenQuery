Inception (2010) is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan, starring Leonardo DiCaprio.
Interstellar (2014) was directed by Christopher Nolan and explores time dilation and black holes.
The Matrix (1999) introduced the concept of simulated reality and stars Keanu Reeves as Neo.
Avatar (2009) directed by James Cameron is set on the alien world Pandora.
The Martian (2015) directed by Ridley Scott follows astronaut Mark Watney stranded on Mars.
Dune (2021) directed by Denis Villeneuve is based on the novel by Frank Herbert.
